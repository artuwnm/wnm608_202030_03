24 Solar Terms is a theme store located in downtown San Francisco, USA. The theme of the store is 24 solar terms, with the goal of cultural inheritance and integration into contemporary life. It integrates traditional culture with modern aesthetics to provide customers with a selection of specialty products, such as calendars, clocks, canvas bags, and so on.

The 24 solar terms is a specific knowledge continuum of time for Chinese people. Ancient Chinese divided the circle of the annual motion of the sun into 24 equal segments and each segment was called a “jie qi”of solar term. Hence the 24 segments are collectively called the Twenty-four Solar Terms.
