Balsamic Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

1 slice of NY Cheesecake, Balsamic Vinegar, Sugar, Strawberries, Whipped Cream

Directions

Step 1: Combine the strawberries, balsamic vinegar and sugar in a bowl. Mix and let stand for a syrup to form.

Step 2: Plate 1 slice of NY Cheesecake.

Step 3: Spoon balsamic strawberries on top of the cheesecake.

Step 4: Top with a dollop of freshly whipped cream.
