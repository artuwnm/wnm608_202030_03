The increasing population of homeless pets has become a serious issue in urban area where these little creatures have to struggle for food and breeding.They suffer from pollutions, which are likely to cause the propagation of infectious diseases. As a result, we have founded PETOPIA to help our homeless friends.We provide homes for them with professional health and life care, and meanwhile they serve as intimate partners to their human friends before starting their new life with new families.
