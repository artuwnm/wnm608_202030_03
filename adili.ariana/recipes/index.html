MINIMAL RECIPES

Breakfast

CINNAMON-BANANA-PEANUT BUTTER TOAST

Items needed

  • Bread of your choice
  • Peanut spread (creamy or crunchy)
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Banana

Preparation

  • Toast each slice of bread in your toaster until golden brown
  • Lather your toasted slices with peanut spread
  • Slice banana into circles
  • Gently sprinkle cinnamon powder over the top

Lunch

CAESAR SALAD

Items needed

  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Herbed garlic and parmesan croutons

Preparation

  • Wash lettuce under cold water
  • Chop lettuce into small, thick strips and toss into a bowl
  • Pour less than a cup of caesar dressing over lettuce and mix around
  • Top salad with herbed croutons
  • Grate parmesan cheese over the top

Dinner

FETTUCCINE ALFREDO

Items needed

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Alfredo pasta sauce
  • Grated parmesan
  • Ground black pepper
  • Chopped parsley

Preparation

  • Boil salted water at high heat
  • When bubbles surface, add noodles in the water
  • Wait 10-13 minutes for noodles to soften
  • Drain all water from noodles
  • Add alfredo sauce to noodles and stir
  • Plate your pasta and top with parsley and grated parmesan
