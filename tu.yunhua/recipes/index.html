Ingredients

sea cucumber 1 scallops 2 shrimps 10 Salt amount Ground pepper Winter vegetables
Shallots Ginger shreds Coriander / Celery A cup of rice

1 Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.

2 Remove the shrimp head, open the sides, remove the intestines, wash them for later use; do not throw the shrimp head.

3 Shrimp head uses kitchen paper to absorb surface moisture, put oil in the pan, fry the shrimp head, and drain the shrimp oil.

4 Wash the scallops for later use and tear them apart; slice the sea cucumber and dice for later; shred ginger, chopped coriander or parsley, and chopped shallots;

5 Soaked rice, add water to casserole; turn to medium-low heat after boiling over high heat, slowly boil for 20 minutes;

6 During the rice boil until flowering, add scallops, add shrimp sauce, 4-5 scoops according to your preference; then add ginger shreds.

7 Six minutes before turning off the heat, add shrimp, sea cucumber, and finally salt and pepper, sprinkle with scallion and celery.

8 Sea cucumber and shrimp casserole porridge is finished.

Ingredients

Filet Mignon 500g A little black pepper Spoonful of salt 1 Spoons of olive oil 2 Yellow Mustard 30g Egg yolk 1
Tricholoma 350g Mushroom 150g Three cloves of garlic Half a spoon of soy sauce Parma ham 6 slices Three spoons of bread crumbs

1 First place the filet mignon at room temperature, blot the surface moisture with kitchen paper, then apply salt and black pepper for a simple seasoning.

2 Pour olive oil into the pan. After the pan and oil are heated, place the salted and black pepper-stewed steak in the pan and fry. This step is to make Maillard reaction on the steak surface and increase the aroma. The steak is fried for about 45 seconds on each side, and 6 sides are to be fried. You can adjust the time according to the color of the surface.

3 After the steak is cooked, remove it from the pan, brush the yellow mustard sauce while it is hot, and set aside. The oil in the pan is left in the pan for use.

4 Cut the garlic, mushrooms and shiitake into small cubes, as small as possible, put them in the pan where you just fry the steak and fry with a base oil over low heat. Dry the mushrooms to prevent water from coming out when grilling. You can add a little soy sauce to increase the flavor during frying.

5 Put a layer of plastic wrap on a clean table, and wrap the parma ham slices on the plastic wrap. The length should be longer than the circumference of the steak to ensure that it covers the steak. Spreading cold mushroom sauce on the ham slices can also cover the steak.

6 After laying the mushroom sauce, sprinkle a layer of bread crumbs at the place where the steak is to be placed, which can absorb the gravy that precipitated during roasting, and ensure the integrity and taste of the meringue. Place the steak, roll it up, make sure the ham completely wraps the steak, and wrap it tightly in plastic wrap.

7 Tighten both sides as well, and put them in the refrigerator freezer for 15 minutes.

8 At this time, prepare meringues (you can buy frozen meringues in the supermarket). Spread the meringues at room temperature and roll them thin. Take out the shaped ham steak rolls. , Make sure that the meringue completely wraps the steak roll once, cut off the excess meringue, pinch the bottom and both ends to the seal. Wrap it in plastic wrap, wrap it tightly, and refrigerate in the refrigerator for more than an hour (no problem overnight in the refrigerator)..

9 Refrigerate and take it out. Prepare 1 egg yolk to break up and brush on the surface of the meringue. Then draw the pattern you like on the back of the knife, and the steak is ready to be done. Be careful not to cut through the meringue.

10 Spread baking paper on the baking sheet and place the patterned Wellington steak on the baking paper. After preheating in the oven at 225 degrees, place the steaks in the oven. Bake at 225 degrees for 40 minutes. Take it out for 5 minutes and you can enjoy it.

11 In this way, the Wellington steak is completed, and it is cooked to medium for 40 minutes. After cutting, you can see the pink and tender strength. If you like steak with a higher degree of maturity, you can cook for 5 minutes longer.
